Fog City Trail

Step through Pacifica, coast to ridge

The Fog City Trail is a proposed end-to-end route through Pacifica, California — in the spirit of the San Francisco Crosstown Trail. Running the length of the city from the bluffs of Mussel Rock to the wild canyons of San Pedro Valley, it links neighborhood streets and staircases, the existing waterfront trail, coastal bluffs, and (with an optional spur) the high ridgeline of Montara Mountain.

Walk it, run it, or bike it — in either direction. Cross the city in a day, or explore it one section at a time.
